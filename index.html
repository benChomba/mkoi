Among the many great sons of Kilifi County is Stewart Madzayo Mwachiru, fondly referred to as Mkoi Wehu.
Over the years, he has dutifully and diligently served as:

  • The Deputy Minority Leader
  • An advocate of the high court
  • High Court judge
  • Member of the Pan-African Parliament
